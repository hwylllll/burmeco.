Inlay Cotton :

Origin: Inlay

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Chemical-dyed

Texture: Soft, may start slightly rough

Price Range: 6,000–7,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Made from lotus stems (a renewable resource), zero chemicals, anti-bacterial, no electricity used

Extra Info: This fabric requires less knitting, making it a more eco-friendly choice. Lotus fiber is carefully hand-pulled from each stem, with over 10,000 stems needed to create just one scarf. Additionally, it is used to weave silk and cotton longyis adorned with traditional regional Intha patterns.
