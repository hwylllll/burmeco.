Scoured Inlay Cotton :

Origin: Inlay

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Chemical-dyed

Texture: Softens with use; initially slightly rough but doesn’t shrink

Price Range: Above 40,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Handwoven using natural fibers, low energy use, biodegradable

Extra Info: Unlike regular Inlay cotton, this fabric doesn’t shrink thanks to a thorough scouring process, which also helps natural dyes bond better and improves colorfastness. It is washed using only water or natural soap, making it chemical-free and safe for waterways.
