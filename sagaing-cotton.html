Sagaing Cotton :

Origin: Sagaing

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Natural-dyed

Texture: Soft and slightly thicker than Minbuu cotton

Price Range: 3,000–5,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Uses plant-based dyes, often made in monasteries or small communities

Extra Info: This fabric is commonly used in everyday wear and robes for monks, reflecting Buddhist values of simplicity. It is also more durable and retains its shape better even after multiple washes.
