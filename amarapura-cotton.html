Amarapura Cotton :

Origin: Amarapura

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Mostly chemical-dyed

Texture: Slightly glossy, refined

Price Range: Above 6,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Handwoven (no machines), uses local silk/cotton, supports traditional livelihoods

Extra Info: Available in both handwoven and machine-made varieties, the patterns remain unique to each weaver, promoting creativity and preserving cultural heritage. Waste silk threads are often repurposed into smaller accessories, ensuring nothing goes to waste.
