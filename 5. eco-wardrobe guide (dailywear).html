Daily Wear

100-Year Cotton

Origin: Myintkyinar

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Mostly chemical-dyed

Texture: Smooth and light on the skin

Price Range: Above 4,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Natural and biodegradable

Why ideal: 100-year cotton is ideal for daily wear due to its exceptional longevity and refined comfort. Engineered from premium long-staple fibers, it maintains its softness and breathability over time.

Sagaing Cotton

Origin: Sagaing

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Natural-dyed

Texture: Soft and slightly thicker than Minbuu cotton

Price Range: 3,000–5,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Uses plant-based dyes, often made in monasteries or small communities

Why ideal: Sagaing cotton is well-suited for daily wear thanks to its natural breathability and soft texture. Grown in Myanmar’s fertile Sagaing region, it offers lightweight comfort in warm climates.

Marsayite Cotton

Origin: Amarapura

Material: Cotton mixed with silon

Dye Type: Natural-dyed

Texture: Smooth, lightweight, and silky to the touch

Price Range: 20,000–40,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Made with natural fibers, supports ethnic traditions, low-waste production

Why ideal: Marsayite cotton is ideal for daily wear due to its unique blend of softness and resilience. Cultivated in select regions with mineral-rich soil, it delivers a smooth, breathable texture.
