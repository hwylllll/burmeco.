Crafting the future with sustainable traditions.

Our objective

Our mission is to preserve Myanmar's textile heritage and promote sustainable practices, empowering local artisans and integrating eco-friendly materials. We aim to offer products with cultural significance and environmental benefits. Therefore, we specially went to Bogyoke Market in order to interview the shopkeepers that sell Burmese traditional fabrics.
