Marsayite Cotton :

Origin: Amarapura

Material: Cotton mixed with silon

Dye Type: Natural-dyed

Texture: Smooth, lightweight, and silky to the touch

Price Range: 20,000–40,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Made with natural fibers, supports ethnic traditions, low-waste production

Extra Info: Commonly machine-woven, this fabric is popular for modern-style longyis and casual wear. It is crafted by ethnic groups such as the Shan, Pa-O, Palaung, and others. The patterns often reflect their identity, beliefs, and stories passed down through generations.
