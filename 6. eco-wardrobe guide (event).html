Event Wear

Amarapura Cotton

Origin: Amarapura

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Mostly chemical-dyed

Texture: Slightly glossy, refined

Price Range: Above 6,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Handwoven (no machines), uses local silk/cotton, supports traditional livelihoods

Why ideal: Amarapura cotton is perfect for event wear due to its refined texture and elegant drape. The natural dyes enhance its rich colors, while the fabric maintains breathability and comfort throughout extended wear, making it a sophisticated yet practical choice for special occasions.

Inlay Cotton

Origin: Inlay

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Chemical-dyed

Texture: Soft, may start slightly rough

Price Range: 6,000–7,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Made from lotus stems (a renewable resource), zero chemicals, anti-bacterial, no electricity used

Why ideal: Inlay cotton is an excellent choice for event wear because of its elegant texture and subtle shine. Its lightweight yet structured fabric ensures comfort and sophistication, suitable for formal occasions that demand both style and breathability.

Scoured Inlay Cotton

Origin: Inlay

Material: Cotton

Dye Type: Chemical-dyed

Texture: Softens with use; initially slightly rough but doesn’t shrink

Price Range: Above 40,000 MMK / yard

Sustainability: Handwoven using natural fibers, low energy use, biodegradable

Why ideal: Scoured Inlay cotton is ideal for event wear due to its refined softness and clean finish achieved through scouring. The fabric’s enhanced purity improves dye absorption and breathability, creating a luxurious, comfortable material perfect for elegant occasions.
